# CAffNet Claim 2 — Joint Optimization Evidence

Fully trainable `f_theta` and `w_phi` networks were optimized jointly through the CAffine layer on an input-dependent rotating safety-control affine face. Five seeds reproduce machine-precision feasibility and a large advantage over applying orthogonal projection only after unconstrained training. A fixed orthogonal projection trained end-to-end also reaches the oracle, qualifying any stronger uniqueness claim. See `summary.json`, `learning_curves.csv`, `run.log`, and `verification.json`.
